# 📌 Project Overview

The **Retail Sales & Inventory Intelligence System** is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution that integrates **Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI** to analyze sales performance, inventory, customer behavior, product trends, and staff productivity.

The project transforms raw retail data into interactive dashboards and actionable business insights that help improve sales, inventory management, and operational efficiency.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Retail businesses often struggle to answer questions such as:

- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- Which stores perform best?
- How can inventory be optimized?
- Who are the most valuable customers?
- Which employees contribute the most sales?
- What causes delayed shipments?
- Which products should be restocked?

# 📑 Dashboard Pages

## 📊 Sales Performance

### KPIs

- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Discount
- Average Order Value

### Insights

- Baldwin Bikes generated the highest sales.
- Sales declined from Q1 to Q4.
- Nearly one-third of shipments were delayed.

---

## 🚴 Product Analysis

### KPIs

- Top Selling Product
- Total Units Sold
- Profit Margin
- Average Unit Price

### Insights

- Trek Slash 8 27.5 - 2016 was the highest revenue product.
- Mountain Bikes generated the largest share of revenue.
- Some products showed unusually high negative sales requiring investigation.

---

## 📦 Inventory & Stock Monitor

### KPIs

- Total Inventory
- Low Stock Count
- Stock Value
- Store Inventory

### Insights

- Trek accounted for the largest inventory value.
- Inventory distribution varied significantly between stores.
- Only a small percentage of products were in low stock.

---

## 👥 Customer Insights

### KPIs

- Total Customers
- Total Spend
- Repeat Customer %
- Average Order Value

### Insights

- New York generated the highest customer revenue.
- Customer orders peaked during April.
- Revenue depended heavily on a small group of high-value customers.

---

## 👨‍💼 Staff Performance

### KPIs

- Staff Revenue
- Average Orders
- Average Handling Days
- Average Discounts

### Insights

- Marcelene Boyer and Venita Daniel were the top performers.
- Baldwin Bikes consistently outperformed other stores.
- Staff productivity declined after April.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- Baldwin Bikes generated the highest revenue.
- Trek products dominated total sales.
- Mountain Bikes were the most profitable category.
- New York represented the largest customer market.
- Inventory levels remained healthy across stores.
- High-value customers contributed the majority of revenue.
- Shipment delays affected nearly one-third of orders.
- Sales showed clear seasonal patterns.

---

# 📈 Business Recommendations

- Expand inventory for top-selling products.
- Reduce stock for slow-moving products.
- Improve logistics to reduce shipment delays.
- Reward top-performing employees.
- Increase promotions in low-performing regions.
- Launch loyalty programs for repeat customers.
- Forecast demand using historical sales trends.
